/* WHAT WE DO */

Ludwitt is an AI-powered adaptive learning platform designed to address
the alarming decline in adult literacy and numeracy skills documented by
PIAAC (Programme for the International Assessment of Adult Competencies).

We serve learners of ALL ages:
- K-12 students building foundational skills
- Adults rebuilding literacy and numeracy capabilities
- Families learning together
- Educators seeking effective supplemental tools
- Workforce development programs

/* THE PROBLEM WE'RE SOLVING */

PIAAC 2023 reveals a crisis:
- US adult literacy scores dropped 12 points (271→258) from 2017-2023
- US adult numeracy scores dropped 7 points (255→249) from 2017-2023
- 28% of US adults now struggle with basic literacy (up from 19%)
- 34% of US adults now struggle with basic numeracy (up from 29%)
- Approximately 54 million Americans struggle with basic reading
- Approximately 66 million Americans struggle with basic math

This isn't just a US problem—most OECD countries show similar trends.

/* LEARNING SCIENCE FOUNDATIONS */

Our approach is grounded in research:

1. Zone of Proximal Development (Vygotsky)
   - Keep learners optimally challenged

2. Spaced Repetition (Ebbinghaus)
   - Review at optimal intervals for retention

3. Mastery Learning (Bloom)
   - Demonstrate competence before advancing

4. Feedback Impact (Hattie)
   - Immediate, specific feedback on every problem

5. Growth Mindset (Dweck)
   - Emphasize effort and improvement
